Raha International School - Gardens Campus is an IB school located in Raha Gardens, Abu Dhabi. With age ranges of between 4 and 18 years, Raha is prepared to support your child for many years to come. It has achieved an outstanding rating by consistently helping their students reach excellence, both academically and personally. Furthermore, the prestigious IB system of education promotes vital skills including critical thinking, individuality and neuroplasticity. Students at Raha are motivated to constantly improve themselves by learning new things, challenging their preconceptions and ideas, as well as collaborating with their peers. Raha promotes equality, in all social facets. We make sure that all of our students are comfortable at the school, and feel safe. Here at Raha, we make sure that you children recieve the best education, with facilities such as the science labs, gym and pool. Located in the Raha Gardens community, the school environment couldn't be friendlier! The students at Raha foster their own community of social care, making sure that everyone has someone they can talk to in a time of need.
